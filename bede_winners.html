Bede Winners

   The "Bede Gold Medal" is the highest and most prestigious award that can be bestowed on an Antonian. The medal, was originally presented by John Barnet Esq., in 1939, to the 'Most Outstanding All-Round Student' in memory of Right Reverend Dr. Dom Bede Beeckmeyer, D.D., OSB, to whom St. Anthony`s College will be ever grateful for Purchasing the site of the present home at Katugastota.
